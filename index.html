OUR JOURNEY BEGINS.

Saleem Construction Company was founded in 1999 by M.Saleem with a vision to redefine the construction industry. M.Saleem had a deep passion for building structures that stand the test of time, not just in terms of physical endurance, but also in the hearts of people who use them. With this vision in mind, M.Saleem gathered a team of dedicated professionals, each bringing unique skills and expertise to the table.
